# GST Registration Guide 2026

GST registration is one of the first compliance requirements for many businesses operating in India. Once registered, a business receives a unique GST Identification Number (GSTIN) and becomes legally eligible to collect GST from customers and claim Input Tax Credit (ITC).

This guide explains who needs GST registration, the documents required, registration fees, timelines, and the complete online process.

---

## What is GST Registration?

GST Registration is the process of enrolling a business under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) system. After approval, the business receives a GSTIN which must be quoted on invoices, returns, and tax-related documents.

A GSTIN is a 15-digit identification number linked to the PAN of the business.

---

## Who Must Register for GST?

GST registration is mandatory in several situations:

### Businesses Crossing the Threshold Limit

Businesses whose aggregate annual turnover exceeds the prescribed threshold limit must register.

### Interstate Suppliers

Businesses supplying goods or services across state boundaries may require GST registration.

### E-Commerce Sellers

Businesses selling through marketplaces may need GST registration depending on applicable regulations.

### Input Tax Credit Benefits

Many businesses voluntarily register to claim Input Tax Credit and improve business credibility.

---

## Benefits of GST Registration

Registering under GST offers several advantages:

* Legal recognition as a supplier
* Ability to collect GST from customers
* Claim Input Tax Credit on purchases
* Improved business credibility
* Easier participation in B2B transactions
* Better compliance with government regulations

## Documents Required for GST Registration

Before starting the application, keep scanned copies of the following documents ready.

### Individual / Proprietorship

* PAN Card
* Aadhaar Card
* Passport-sized Photograph
* Bank Account Statement or Cancelled Cheque
* Business Address Proof

### Partnership Firm

* PAN Card of Firm
* Partnership Deed
* Address Proof
* Bank Details
* Authorized Signatory Documents

### Private Limited Company / LLP

* Certificate of Incorporation
* PAN Card
* Memorandum & Articles of Association
* Board Resolution / Authorization Letter
* Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
* Registered Office Proof

## GST Registration Fees

GST registration on the government portal is completely free.

However, businesses may choose to hire tax consultants or Chartered Accountants to assist with documentation and filing, which can involve professional service charges.

## Step-by-Step GST Registration Process

### Step 1: Visit the GST Portal

Open the official GST portal and select the option for New Registration.

### Step 2: Complete Part A

Provide:

* PAN Number
* Mobile Number
* Email Address

Verify the OTP sent to your mobile and email.

A Temporary Reference Number (TRN) will be generated.

### Step 3: Complete Part B

Log in using the TRN and enter:

* Business details
* Promoter details
* Authorized signatory details
* Principal place of business
* Bank account information

Upload all required supporting documents.

### Step 4: Submit Application

Submit the application using:

* Aadhaar Authentication
* EVC
* DSC (where applicable)

An Application Reference Number (ARN) will be generated.

### Step 5: Verification by GST Officer

The application is reviewed by GST authorities.

If additional information is required, a notice may be issued requesting clarification.

### Step 6: GSTIN Issued

Upon successful verification, a GST Registration Certificate and GSTIN are issued.

---

## How Long Does GST Registration Take?

In most cases:

* Aadhaar-authenticated applications are processed faster.
* Applications requiring officer verification may take longer.

Businesses should regularly monitor application status using the ARN.

---

## Common Reasons for GST Registration Rejection

Applications are commonly rejected due to:

* Incorrect PAN details
* Mismatched Aadhaar information
* Invalid address proof
* Poor-quality document uploads
* Incorrect business information

Always verify information before submission.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is GST registration free?

Yes. The government does not charge any registration fee.

### Can I register voluntarily?

Yes. Businesses below the threshold limit can register voluntarily.

### Is GST registration mandatory for freelancers?

It depends on turnover, nature of services, and place of supply rules.

### Can I apply without Aadhaar?

Yes, but additional verification procedures may apply.

### How can I check GST registration status?

You can track application status using the ARN generated after submission.
