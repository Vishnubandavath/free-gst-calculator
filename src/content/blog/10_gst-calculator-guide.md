# GST Rates in India

GST rates vary according to product and service classifications.

| GST Rate | Common Usage            |
| -------- | ----------------------- |

# GST Inclusive vs GST Exclusive Calculations

Understanding the difference between inclusive and exclusive pricing is critical.

---

## GST Exclusive Price

The displayed amount excludes GST.

GST is added separately.

### Example

Product Price:

₹10,000

GST Rate:

18%

GST Amount:

₹1,800

Final Invoice Value:

₹11,800

---

## GST Inclusive Price

The displayed amount already includes GST.

The GST portion must be extracted from the total amount.

### Example

Invoice Amount:

₹11,800

GST Rate:

18%

Taxable Value:

₹10,000

GST Amount:

₹1,800

# GST Formula Explained

A GST Calculator uses standard GST formulas.

---

## GST Exclusive Formula

To calculate GST on an exclusive amount:

```text
GST Amount = Taxable Value × GST Rate ÷ 100
```

---

## Final Invoice Formula

```text
Invoice Value = Taxable Value + GST Amount
```

---

## GST Inclusive Formula

To extract GST from an inclusive amount:

```text
Taxable Value = Inclusive Amount ÷ (1 + GST Rate/100)
```

---

## GST Extraction Formula

```text
GST Amount = Inclusive Amount − Taxable Value
```

These formulas are the foundation of all GST calculators.
